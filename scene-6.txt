Harry Programmer and the Sorcerer's Code: Scene 6
It's sorting hat day! Without hesitation, Hermione is selected as a Ravenclaw. Ron, though terrified becomes a Gryffindor. Draco Malfoy becomes a proud Slytherin, just like his father and grandfather before him. The sorting hat stumbles at first with Harry, first hissing out a long s before deciding, finally, that Harry will proudly join the house of Gryffindor.
